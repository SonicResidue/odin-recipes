Chicago Style Italian Roast Beef Sandwich

Description

This quick and easy "meal in a kit" will give you that taste of Chicago you've been craving!

Ingredients (Note: This kit has everything you need!)

  • 2 lbs. Sliced Italian beef
  • 2 lbs. gravy
  • 6 oz. Giardiniera
  • 4 French rolls

Steps

  1. Pour gravy into medium pan, simmer on medium heat
  2. Pull apart beef, place in pan and simmer until hot. ABout 5 minutes.
  3. Open rolls, place beef inside, and ladle gravy
  4. Garnish with giardiniera.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
