E-Z Chicken Pot Pie

Description

Looking for a warm easy to make comfort meal? Look no further! This delicious chicken pot pie recipe is easy and satisfying. Everything can be made in under an hour!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • 2 frozen pie crusts
  • 2 oz cheddar cheese
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 small can sliced mushrooms
  • 1 small can peas and carrots

Steps

  1. Take pie crust out of freezer, allow to thaw slightly.
  2. Cut chicken into small pieces, and pan cook until done.
  3. In a large bowl, mix chicken, soup, vegetables and cheese, then pour evenly into crust.
  4. Remove other crust from pan, place upside down on bottom crust, and crimp edges.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 350 degrees for thirty minutes.
  6. Remove from oven, let cool slightly, then slice and serve!
