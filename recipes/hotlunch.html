Hot Lunch (Warning: It's Not What You Think!)

Description

Up for a challenge? Feeling invincible? This flaming concoction is red hot, and only the strongest of souls can take the heat! (WARNING: You probably can't take it, so don't try.)

Ingredients

  • 2 oz rum
  • 1 dash tabasco sauce
  • 1 small pickle slice

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a cocktail glass, garnish with pickle.
  2. Light contents on fire.
  3. Tilt head back and have your buddy carefuly pour it into your mouth.
  4. Have four more and go to bed. You have no business going anywhere!
